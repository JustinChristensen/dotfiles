Converting decimal to binary

14.75

1. Separate the fractional part from the whole part
14.75 - 14 = 0.75

2. Convert the whole number to binary
14 = 0b1110

3. Multiply by 2 and continue converting the whole number part to binary
0.75 = 3/4

3/4 * 2 = 6/4   1
2/4 * 2 = 4/4   1

4. Result
1110.11
